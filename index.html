🔎 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers impersonate trusted entities to steal sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data. They often use fake websites, emails, or messages that appear legitimate.

🚫 What It Can Cause

  • Identity theft
  • Loss of bank or personal information
  • Malware infection
  • Unauthorized access to accounts

✅ How to Stay Safe

  • Always verify URLs before clicking
  • Look for HTTPS and proper domains
  • Never enter sensitive info on suspicious sites
  • Use tools like Zyqox to scan unknown URLs
